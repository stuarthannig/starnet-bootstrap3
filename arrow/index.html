Welcome to Arrow – Prefabricated Pumping Stations

Arrow prefabricated “walk-in” pumping stations are available in several pre-engineered models as well as a customizable version. Using standard designs allows us to pre-build and stock (contact manufacturer for specific availability) which makes turnaround fast. Our product requires minimal field work upon delivery.

Arrow Model L55 Arrow Model L88 Arrow Model G1020V Arrow Model MCX

Our unique designs eliminate many safety hazards such as confined space, fall hazards and weather related dangers. Our control panel design greatly simplifies compliance with electrical safety regulations (CFR 1910 Subpart S) and standards (NFPA 70E). The compartmentalization of the ArcSafe® panel keeps line voltage away from anyone who may need to get into cabinets. The building, arrives assembled, with control components and wiring pre-installed.

ArcSafe® Pump Control Center (PCC)

Most of our standard models include the ArcSafe® PCC, as manufactured by Starnet Technologies, Inc. This system allows you to maintain “IN AUTO” operation while servicing any of the line voltage compartments de-energized, making compliance with NFPA70E and OSHA 1910 simple.
